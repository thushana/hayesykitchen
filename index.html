Sugar

Bacon Bourbon Brownies

5.10.2016 - San Francisco

I used to live a block away from where I do now, also on Hayes Street, in an apartment that shared a fire escape with one of the neighboring units. Our floor plans were mirror images of each other and our kitchens had a full size door and a full size window that opened out onto the fire escape.

My neighbors were lovely people. One in particular. Fascinating, more like it. A Sri Lankan born, Amherst, MA raised computer scientist who thought he knew a thing or two about design. His insatiable curiosity, big brown eyes, and a smile so sparkling white it glowed against his chocolate skin certainly caught my attention; not to mention his everyday-cowboy boots and shiny belt buckle style. I did my best to play it cool.

At this same time, I was also working part-time for a friend who had started his own food truck. I was able to make and sell desserts for the truck, which meant several recipe-testings and numerous batches to make. Which meant I needed taste-testers. Which meant I had a valid reason to constantly invite the browncowboy over to give me an honest opinion. Come to find out, the amount of opinions he has to give is rivaled only by amount of questions he has to ask, and sparkling smiles he has to offer.

And, come to find out, that smile is filled with 28 sweet-teeth. Lucky for me, it wasn't that hard to convince him to taste a few recipes. Lucky for him, his neighbor had plenty of cups of sugar in the cupboard already.

It was only a couple weeks in when I learned of his love for chocolate...
